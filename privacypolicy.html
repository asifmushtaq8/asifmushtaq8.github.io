Cookies

Cookies are used by Phantom Studio. A cookie, which is a small piece of data or a text file, is downloaded to your computer or mobile device when you visit certain websites. Cookies may contain text that can be read by the web server that sent the cookie to you. The cookie text is normally a series of characters and numbers that uniquely identifies your computer or mobile device, but it may also contain additional information. By agreeing to our use of non-essential cookies, you grant us and the third parties with whom we collaborate permission to place, store, and access some or all of the cookies described below on your device.

Persistent Cookies

– These cookies are stored on a user’s device between browser sessions, which allows the user’s preferences or actions across a website or across different websites to be remembered. Persistent cookies may be used for several purposes, including remembering users' preferences when using Phantom Studio or to target advertising to them.

Session Cookies

– These cookies allow Phantom Studio to link the actions of a user during a browsing session. They may be used for a variety of purposes, such as remembering what a user has purchased as they browse Phantom Studio. Session cookies also permit users to be recognized as they navigate Phantom Studio so that any item or page changes they make are memorized from page to page. Session cookies expire after a browser session; they are not saved long term.

Performance Cookies

–These cookies track information on how Phantom Studio is used, including pages visited, traffic sources, user interests, content management, and other metrics.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

– These cookies are required for Phantom Studio to work properly, including displaying content, logging in, authenticating your session, responding to service requests, and other purposes. The usage of cookies can be disabled in most web browsers. You may not be able to access Phantom Studio features correctly or at all if you disable certain cookies.

Functional Cookies

– These cookies allow Phantom Studio to remember user preferences such as language, ids, and other preferences while using the site. They can also be used to provide services, such as allowing users to write blog posts, listen to audio, or watch videos on Phantom Studio.
